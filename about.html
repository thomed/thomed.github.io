About Me

Although my work experience keeps me close to the JVM, my favorite programming language is C. Now that I'm finished with my degree, I'm looking forward to applying my skills in a more professional capacity as well as exploring personal projects and hobbies.

Outside of CS and programming, some things I enjoy are skiing, reading, making music, and playing video games.

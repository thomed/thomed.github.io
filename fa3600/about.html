This is a work of fiction

loosely based on various historical events.

This interactive experience by Thomas Walker was done for a class at the University of Utah.

It puts you in the (fictional) shoes of a hacker from the 1980s. This was a time when hackers were feared, in part due to movies like WarGames.

It is not 100% accurate.
For example, the terminal window is based off of more modern shells and was designed to be accessible to those not overly familiar with a command-line.

To be clear, no one is actually connecting to your computer and all code is being run on your machine.
